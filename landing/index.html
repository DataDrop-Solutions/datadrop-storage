FAQ

Common questions.

Why DataDrop instead of Google Drive or Dropbox?

They sell fixed storage plans — 100 GB, 2 TB — whether you use the space or not. If you store 22 GB, you still pay for the full plan tier.

DataDrop charges only for what you keep, measured by the second. It also includes a zero-knowledge Vault and encrypted team Workspaces that competitors don't offer.

How does the monthly limit work?

Your monthly limit determines the maximum storage capacity available to your account. A ₹149/mo limit = 100 GB max storage. A ₹298/mo limit = 200 GB. This capacity stays the same throughout the billing cycle — it does not shrink based on the date.

Your actual bill is calculated continuously based on how long your files remain stored, so uploading near the end of the month costs less than storing the same files for a full month. At month end, your exact usage is collected via UPI AutoPay.

How does billing work?

DataDrop tracks every byte stored, measured by the second, at ₹1.49 per GB per month. Store 100 GB for a full month = ₹149. Store the same 100 GB for only half the month = ₹74.50.

At the end of each billing cycle, only your actual usage is charged via UPI AutoPay — never more than your approved limit. Uploads are blocked if your total stored files would exceed your capacity limit.

What happens when the free trial ends?

Your account becomes read-only. You can still view and download all your files — nothing is deleted immediately. Your files are kept for 30 days from your account creation date (15 days after the trial ends) before any removal.

To resume uploads, set up UPI AutoPay and configure a monthly spending limit. See our Terms of Service for the full lifecycle policy.

Can DataDrop read my Vault files?

No. Vault files are encrypted on your device before upload using keys only you hold. Our servers receive and store only ciphertext. We have no mechanism to decrypt your Vault — including our own engineering team.

If you forget your PIN, only the recovery phrase you created at Vault setup can restore access.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no annual commitments, no cancellation fees. During the trial, no payment is required. After the trial, billing is month-to-month based on usage. See our Refund Policy for full details.

What happens if a payment fails?

If UPI AutoPay fails, your account moves to read-only status. Your files remain safe and accessible to download. You receive email reminders at 7, 21, and 30 days. Files are permanently deleted only after 35 days of non-payment, with advance warning. See the Refund Policy for the full timeline.

Why are there three separate storage types?

Files — everyday storage with flexible sharing, like Google Drive but pay-per-use.

Vault — personal encrypted space only you can open. Use it for sensitive documents, private records, anything you don't want anyone else to access.

Workspace — encrypted team collaboration. Only invited members can access files; each member holds their own key.

Is DataDrop available on mobile?

DataDrop is a progressive web app that works on all modern browsers — mobile and desktop — with no installation required. All features including the Vault and Workspace are accessible from any device.
